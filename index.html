Every one is essentially the character of his or her thoughts and that explains why people differ in their behavior. As a man thinks in his heart, so is he. In other words, you’re not different from the way you think. Through months and years of continuously thinking in a certain way, your mind becomes programmed in that direction, and that’s the direction your life will go. Every thing you can see today started from what you desire-what you thought. Your thought is a unique product of your creation. Creation must first of all start from thinking; thinking of what to create, how to create it and when to create it. Simply put, you create things by merely thinking about it. The truth is, you are your thoughts, positively or negatively. Your thought rules you. When you allow the right thoughts to dwell and envelop your mind, that’s what will make you a success in all ramifications of life. I dare you to think differently, Act differently and be differently different!
